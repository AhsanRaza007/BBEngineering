PTFE Nozzles

PTFE Nozzles are effectively used in different business such as:

  • petrochemicals
  • engineering
  • beverage industry
  • aeronautics
  • fertilizers

These are designed for containers for simple flow of material such as liquids and semi-liquids.

They are precise diameter for installation and discharge purposes.

More on Nozzles

These are mainly outlined in white color as they suit both the interior and exterior of storage containers.

One end of PTFE Nozzles is completely threaded, which implies they can be easily installed in the manner of screw without any or least tools.

The provided nozzles have small opening at the back so that the contaminants do not flow with the material.
