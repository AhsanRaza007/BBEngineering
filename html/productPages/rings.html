PTFE Rings

While most common Ring materials are rubber or elastomeric compounds, certain operating conditions and hardware configurations merit the use of PTFE as the material.

Advantages over Elastomers

  • corrosion resistance
  • massive temperature range capabilities
  • excellent electrical properties
  • unlimited shelf life

PTFE Rings we make:

  • PTFE O-Rings
  • PTFE Back-up Rings
  • PTFE V-Rings
  • PTFE U-Rings
  • PTFE Piston Rings


PTFE O-Rings

The compound of polymerization is utilized to make our PTFE O-Rings by utilizing hi-tech machines, chemicals, elements, tools, technology and other components.

These rings are broadly utilized in the chemical sector as well as are highly known by the customers for their corrosion resistance, dimensions, elevated tensile strength, performance and reliability.

The provided PTFE O Rings are available in variable diameters, lengths and dimensions.

These have the most differing features that make them to tolerate elevated pressure and temperature conditions.

PTFE Back-up Rings

PTFE Backup rings are manufactured to support & prevent the problems of extrusion of the Rings when it is subject to high pressures in both dynamic and static applications.

Spiral Back-up Rings

Spiral Back-up Rings are most commonly consumed rings and is self adjusting to diametric tolerances. It is basically available in single ordouble turn.

Solid Back-up Rings

Solid Back-up Rings are solid and don't have any cuts and are there just to support or provide fall back to other rings.

SPlit Back-up Rings

Split Back-up Rings are manufactured same as solid back-up rings but is split at 30 Degree to facilitate ease in certain applications


All varieties are processed to suit PTFE Rings standards.

It can be supplied in Virgin PTFE, Glass Filled PTFE, Pigmented PTFE or as per end-user requirements.


PTFE V-Rings

V rings are machined as per customer specifications/drawings.

It can be used on all pressure ranges by varying the number of V-rings in the set It is also known as Chevron Packing

V-Packing which has Cryogenic applications, Chemical application. V-Packing are majorly consumed by Valve engineering


PTFE U-Rings

Rings are processed for high functional reliability & resistance to ageing.

It s reliability consist of high pressures & vacuum used for rotary & static applications.

These Rings are available with springs for seal rotating and translational movements It is also known as PTFE U-packings which has many engineering application in

  • Mechanical engineering
  • Pharmaceuticals
  • Petrochemicals
  • Valve Engineering


PTFE Piston Rings

Piston rings possess the quality to operate without lubrication or in less lubricated conditions.

The function of a piston ring is to guide the piston and piston rod of a cylinder, absorbing transverse forces.

It is a resistance to chemical attacks.

Rider Rings, Wearing Rings, Guide Rings are also manufactured in different grades and also as per customer specification.
